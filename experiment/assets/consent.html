Viral Diagnosis

In this online study you will be asked to go through two phases: one learning phase and one testing phase. In the learning phase you will be shown some mild symptoms presented as text, e.g. “tiredness”, and then sort these symptoms into their respective diseases, which are completely fictional. During this phase, you will also be provided feedback on your accuracy. After a certain number of correct trials are done, you will move on to the testing phase. In the testing phase, you will be asked to sort symptoms into diseases without corrective feedback.

The purpose of the experiment is to study basic processes of learning. There are no foreseeable risks or discomfort associated with completing this study.

You are free to withdraw at any time during the study, any completed tasks until the point of leaving will be deleted and destroyed. You are also free to withdraw your data at any time up until the point of anonymous publication of these data, which will occur at the time the journal article is be published. To withdraw during the experiment, simply close your browser tab.

1 point will be allocated for taking part in the study.
