Thank you for taking part in the study!

The study aimed to see if the inverse base-rate effect was present in this simple experiment. Using the previous work of Kruschke (1996) and Lamberts & Kent (2007), we hypothesise that no inverse base rate will be evident, due to the lack of cognitive load being presented during the study.
